SUPPORT THE EFL BENEVOLENCE FUND

 

The EFL Benevolence Fund exists to extend compassion and restoration to men in moments of real crisis. Through your generosity, we are able to meet immediate needs—providing financial relief, spiritual encouragement, and practical assistance that point directly to the peace and hope found in Christ.

Each gift becomes more than a contribution—it’s a lifeline that demonstrates the tangible love of God in action. By giving generously and stewarding faithfully, you become part of a story of redemption and renewal. Together, we can lift men out of hardship, restore dignity, and watch God move powerfully in their lives.
